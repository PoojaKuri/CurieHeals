Our Products

Helofloxi-OZ

HEALOFLOX-OZ is an antibiotic combination used to treat a wide range of bacterial infections in children. It contains Ofloxacin, a fluoroquinolone antibiotic, and Ornidazole, an anti-protozoal, which work together to target both bacterial and parasitic infections. This combination is commonly used for treating infections in the respiratory tract, gastrointestinal system, urinary tract, and skin.

Heal-A2Z Syrup

Heal-A2Z Syrup is a comprehensive multivitamin, multimineral, and antioxidant supplement designed to bridge nutritional gaps. It supports immunity, neutralizes free radicals, and aids in overall physiological health. Ideal for all age groups, especially beneficial for those recovering from illness, surgery, or injury.

COLDHEAL-FORTE SYRUP

Coldheal-Forte is a stronger formulation of Paracetamol, Phenylephrine HCl, and Chlorpheniramine suspension. It provides balanced relief for severe symptoms of cold and cough, reducing nasal and bronchial congestion. The anti-histaminic properties alleviate constriction and discomfort associated with respiratory issues. Indications include cold, fever, nasal congestion, and allergic rhinitis.
